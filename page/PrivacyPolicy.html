Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome! This Privacy Policy applies to the Rotana.dev website and all Chrome Extensions developed by Kong Rotana (hereafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our"). This includes but is not limited to, the Rotana QR-Barcode Generator, Rotana Password Generator, and any future extensions we may release.

We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. Our fundamental principle is simple: we do not collect, store, share, or process any of your personal data. Our tools are designed to be completely client-side, meaning all operations happen locally on your computer, within your browser.

2. General Principle: No Data Collection

Across our website and all our Chrome Extensions, we adhere to a strict no-data-collection policy. This means:

  • We do not ask for, access, or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or location).
  • We do not collect or track any data you input into our extensions (such as text for QR codes or settings for password generation).
  • We do not monitor your browsing activity or collect any analytics about your usage.

3. How Our Chrome Extensions Work

All our Chrome Extensions are built to be self-contained and operate entirely on your device.

  • Local Processing: Any data you provide to an extension is processed locally by JavaScript running in your browser. For example, when you generate a QR code or a password, the entire process occurs on your machine. No information is ever sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Clipboard Usage: When you use a feature like "copy to clipboard," the extension uses your browser's native clipboard functionality. We do not track or store what you copy.
  • Permissions: We may request minimal permissions required for an extension to function (e.g., `activeTab` to get a page URL). These permissions are used solely for the stated purpose of the feature and are not used to collect data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising services into our website or Chrome Extensions.

Our website may contain links to external sites or services (such as our social media profiles or Telegram bots). This Privacy Policy does not cover those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you choose to visit.

5. Cookies

The Rotana.dev website and our Chrome Extensions do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.
