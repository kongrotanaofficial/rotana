Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Rotana.dev and the Rotana Password Generator Chrome Extension. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information in connection with your use of our website and Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we want to assure you that we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users.

No Data Collection

At Rotana.dev and through the Rotana Password Generator Chrome Extension, we do not collect, store, or share any personal information or data from users. The password generator operates entirely on your device, and no data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How the Password Generator Works

The Rotana Password Generator Chrome Extension generates passwords locally on your device using JavaScript. All processing happens within your browser, and no information is sent to external servers. When you copy a generated password to your clipboard, it is handled by the browser's clipboard API, and no data is stored or tracked by us.

Third-Party Services

Our website and Chrome extension do not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, or services that collect user data. Links to external sites (such as social media or Telegram bots) are provided for your convenience, and we encourage you to review the privacy policies of those services if you choose to interact with them.

Cookies

Rotana.dev does not use cookies or any form of tracking technology on the website or within the Chrome extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
